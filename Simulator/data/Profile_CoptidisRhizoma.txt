[DESCRIPTION]
    https://doi.org/10.1016/j.chroma.2022.463426
    Six protoberberine-type alkaloids, major active alkaloids in Coptidis Rhizoma.
    In retention time order:
        Columbamine (COL)
        Epiberberine (EPI)
        Jatrorrhizine (JAT)
        Coptisine (COP)
        Palmatine (PAL)
        Berberine (BER)
    Two unknown interferences:
        Interference 1, from Coptidis Rhizoma-related topical medication, embedding with COP and overlapped with JAT
        Interference 2, from Chinese patent medicines (Left Metal Pill), overlapped with COP
